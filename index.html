Before You Choose a Paving Contractor, Compare These Six Things.

Two proposals can look similar on price while offering very different levels of preparation, protection and support. This is how we do it.

Written Warranty

Is the applicable protection clearly documented?

At We Love Paving the protection is written into the proposal before work starts. You see what is covered, for how long, and what keeps it valid. Nothing about it is verbal.

Scope Clarity

Are preparation, materials, quantities, exclusions and responsibilities defined?

At We Love Paving prep, materials, quantities, exclusions and who is responsible for what are all listed before you sign, so nothing turns into a change order halfway through.

Eligibility and Conditions

Does the proposal explain what qualifies and what requirements apply?

At We Love Paving you find out what qualifies before the work starts, not after something fails. If your lot needs anything to qualify, that is in the proposal too.

Project Communication

Who handles updates, questions and changes?

At We Love Paving one Paving Advisor owns your project from the walkthrough to the final visit. Same person for updates, questions and changes, not a different number every time.

Scheduling and Operations

How will access, traffic, tenants and business operations be managed?

At We Love Paving we phase the work around your traffic, tenants and business hours, so your property keeps operating while the lot gets fixed.

Post-Project Support

What documentation and protection remain after completion?

At We Love Paving you keep the documentation and the coverage after we leave, and we are the ones who answer the phone if you need us years down the road.
