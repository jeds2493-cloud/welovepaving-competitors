What’s Covered, What Isn’t

The Panda Pledge™ protects qualifying workmanship and structural performance issues while clearly separating covered defects from normal pavement behavior, environmental damage, or improper use.

Coverage is reviewed based on your project tier, approved load capacity, maintenance history, proper use, and inspection findings.

Covered

Not Covered

Covered Under Tier

Eligible issues may qualify when they match the approved project tier.

  • Potholes and base failure on qualifying asphalt installations.
  • Significant settlement, displacement, or heaving on qualifying concrete installations.
  • Structural failures not caused by subgrade conditions outside the original scope of work.
  • Workmanship defects in We Love Paving-installed surfaces.

Not Covered

Some conditions are considered normal wear, external damage, or improper use.

  • Hairline cracks, minor surface fissures, shrinkage cracks, or thermal cracks.
  • Reflective cracking from pre-existing surfaces beneath an overlay.
  • Normal sealcoat fading, oxidation, tire scuffing, or surface wear.
  • Damage from chemical spills, oil leaks, fire, natural disasters, overload, or unauthorized modifications.

What Voids Any Warranty

The Panda Pledge™ depends on proper use, approved maintenance, and compliance with the project’s engineered limits. Certain actions or conditions may void coverage in affected areas or across the project.

Exceeding Load Limits

Operating vehicles or equipment above the approved weight capacity for your project tier may void coverage in affected areas.

Concentrated Heavy Use

Repeated stopping, turning, idling, or heavy loading in concentrated zones, such as dumpster pads, loading docks, fire lanes, or refuse truck areas.

Unauthorized Modifications

Repairs, alterations, trenching, patching, or modifications performed by parties other than We Love Paving or an approved contractor.

Missed Maintenance Cycles

Failure to complete required sealcoating, inspections, or documented maintenance cycles within the required timeframe.

Outstanding Payment Obligations

Undisputed outstanding payment obligations under the service agreement may affect warranty service or claim eligibility.

Warranty eligibility is reviewed based on project records, maintenance history, usage conditions, inspection findings, and the terms applicable to each coverage tier.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Clear answers about The Panda Pledge™, coverage activation, maintenance requirements, and claim process.

What is The Panda Pledge™?

The Panda Pledge™ is We Love Paving’s multi-year commitment to quality, craftsmanship, proactive pavement care, and long-term protection for qualifying projects.

Does every project qualify for 15-year coverage?

No. Up to 15-year pothole-free coverage applies to qualifying full-depth asphalt installations over 1,000 sq. ft. and requires proper activation, approved use, and verified maintenance.

How do I activate the 15-year asphalt coverage?

Coverage requires opt-in election at signing, first-season sealcoating within 6 months, and verified maintenance cycles every 3–4 years.

What maintenance is required to keep coverage active?

Qualifying asphalt projects must follow the required maintenance cycle, including sealcoating, inspections, and documented service records every 3–4 years.

What does the concrete coverage include?

Qualifying concrete installations may include 5-year structural coverage against significant settlement, displacement, or heaving, subject to approved load limits and warranty terms.

How do I file a claim if I find a pothole?

You must notify WLP in writing within 10 business days of discovering the issue by emailing contact@welovepaving.com. We will then perform an inspection to determine eligibility, and if covered, we will repair the pothole promptly at no cost to you.
