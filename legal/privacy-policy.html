At We Love Paving, accessible from https://www.welovepaving.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by We Love Paving and how we use it.

Scope of This Policy
This Privacy Policy describes how we handle personal information collected through our website, including estimate requests, comments, and site survey imagery. Personal information we collect through separate written agreements such as subcontractor onboarding, vendor agreements, or contracts with HOA boards and property managers is governed by the terms of those agreements and any applicable confidentiality provisions, in addition to this policy where relevant.

By browsing our website, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy. Where we ask for your affirmative consent for example, when submitting an estimate request or providing your phone number that consent will be obtained through a separate acknowledgment at the point of collection, in addition to this policy.

Privacy Policy

Our website address is: https://www.welovepaving.com. We are a full-service consulting, design, and build paving company based in Northern California.

Site Imagery: Photos or videos provided for estimates or taken during site surveys are stored to monitor structural integrity and validate warranty coverage over the 15-year maintenance ecosystem. These images may incidentally include nearby vehicles, structures, or individuals present at the site. We do not use incidentally captured third-party imagery for any purpose other than documenting your property’s condition, and we will remove or blur identifiable third-party content upon a verifiable request, where reasonably feasible.

Information We Collect

The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide it.

Site Imagery: Photos or videos provided for estimates or taken during site surveys are stored to monitor structural integrity and validate warranty coverage over the 15-year maintenance ecosystem.

Contact Information: If you contact us directly or request an estimate, we may receive your name, email address, phone number, and the contents of your message.

Comments: When visitors leave comments on the site, we collect the data shown in the comments form, the visitor’s IP address, and browser user agent string to help spam detection.

Media: If you upload images to our website for example, attaching photos to an estimate request these files may contain embedded location data (EXIF/GPS). You do not need to remove this data; we use it to verify the location of your project. This is separate from the photos and videos our team takes during site surveys, which are described under Site Imagery below.

Pavement Condition & Asset Data: To administer your Warranty Program coverage, we collect technical data regarding your property, including pavement square footage, load capacity requirements (ranging from 15,000 to 55,000 lbs), and historical maintenance records.

Device and Interaction Data: To ensure site security, prevent fraud, and improve user experience, we collect device and browser information (such as browser type, operating system, and device identifiers) and aggregated interaction data. Under California law, some of this information may constitute personal information. We may also collect approximate location data (e.g., city or region) derived from your IP address to provide geographically relevant paving services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

Find and prevent fraudulent activities.

Provide, operate, and maintain our paving services.

Improve and personalize your experience on our website.

Communicate with you for customer service, updates, and marketing purposes.

Process your requests for estimates and site surveys.

Service Optimization & Security: We utilize the collected data to securely manage your session, personalize your browsing experience, and connect you more efficiently with our estimating and service teams, ensuring you receive the most relevant information for your project

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law:

Warranty & Pavement Asset Records: retained for a minimum of 15 years to administer Warranty Program coverage.

Estimate & Contact Requests (non-converted leads): retained for up to 3 years from last contact, then deleted unless you become a customer.

Website Comments: retained until removed by you or upon your request.

Device, Log, and Interaction Data: retained for up to 12 months for security and fraud-prevention purposes.

Call and Communication Recordings: retained for up to 1 year for quality assurance and dispute resolution purposes.

We may retain information longer where necessary to comply with a legal obligation, resolve a dispute, or enforce our agreements.

Cookies, Session Storage, and Device Fingerprinting

We use the following categories of cookies and similar technologies:

Necessary Cookies: required for core site functionality and secure form submissions.

Functional Cookies: remember preferences such as previously entered estimate details.

Analytics Cookies: such as Google Analytics, which help us understand site usage in aggregate.

Advertising Cookies: such as the Meta Pixel and Google Ads, used to measure marketing effectiveness and show you relevant ads.

We also use device fingerprinting to detect and prevent fraudulent estimate requests; this data is used only for security purposes, not for advertising.

We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal as a valid opt-out-of-sale/share request for browsers that send it.

Log Files

We Love Paving follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. This includes IP addresses, browser type, and date/time stamps.

Third-Party Data Processors & Cloud Integrations

To ensure rapid response times and seamless project management, the information you submit through our website is securely processed by our trusted third-party operational partners. This includes:

  • Customer Service Infrastructure: We use industry-standard Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems and internal project management boards to assign estimators and schedule your site surveys.
  • Secure Cloud Storage: Site interaction logs and request details are stored in encrypted, enterprise-grade cloud databases to ensure data integrity and improve our services.

We Love Paving does not sell this data. These integrations are used strictly for internal operations, fulfilling your requests, and executing our paving services.

Privacy Rights for California Residents (CPRA/CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA), gives you the following rights with respect to your personal information:

Right to Know: You may request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the categories of sources, our business purpose for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom we have disclosed it, for the preceding 12 months.

Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (for example, information we are required to retain to complete a transaction, comply with a legal obligation, or maintain warranty records).

Right to Correct: You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.

Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do disclose personal information to service providers such as our CRM, project management, and cloud storage providers under written contracts that restrict their use of your information to performing services on our behalf. This type of disclosure is not “selling” or “sharing” under California law and is necessary to schedule your site survey, prepare your estimate, and administer your Warranty Program coverage; it is not something you can opt out of without affecting our ability to provide these services. If we ever begin selling or sharing personal information for advertising purposes, we will update this policy and provide the required opt-out mechanism in advance

Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: You may direct us to limit our use of sensitive personal information, such as precise geolocation, to what is reasonably necessary to perform the services you request. Please note that precise location information is inherent to performing a site survey or estimate; if you limit its use, we may be unable to complete that specific service, though this will not affect any other services or rights.

Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not deny you services, charge you a different price, or provide a different level of quality because you exercised any of these rights.

Right to Appeal: If we deny your request, you may appeal by replying to our denial notice or contacting us using the information below.

How to Submit a Request: You may submit a request by calling us at (888) 273-0077 or emailing contact@welovepaving.com. We will confirm receipt within 10 business days and respond within 45 calendar days, which we may extend once by an additional 45 days with notice to you. We will verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

Communication Monitoring and Recording

We Love Paving may monitor or record telephone calls, emails, text messages, and website form submissions for quality assurance, training, dispute resolution, and service improvement purposes, consistent with California Penal Code Sections 632 and 632.7. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are directed to businesses, property managers, HOAs, schools, and other commercial and institutional clients, and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. When performing site surveys at schools or universities, our data collection is focused on pavement and property conditions, not on students or staff; any incidental capture of individuals is handled as described under Site Imagery above. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at contact@welovepaving.com so we can delete it.

Security Incident Notification

In the event of a security incident affecting your personal information that triggers notification obligations under California Civil Code Section 1798.82 or other applicable law, we will notify affected individuals and any required regulators within the timeframes required by law, and provide information about the incident and steps you can take to protect yourself.
